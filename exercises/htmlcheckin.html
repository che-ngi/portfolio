All About Oshawott!

Oshawott is a starter Pokemon in the Unova region as well as the newly introduced Hisui region. It's a water-type Pokemon that resembles an otter. It is the first evolution of three: Dewott, Samurott. It has a round, spherical white head with triangular blue ears. It has a brown oval shaped nose with freckles covering the cheeks. It has a turquoise body with a scalchop on the belly which Oshawott often uses as a weapon in battle. Oshawott also has padded dark blue feet with three toes as well as a rudder-like tail.

Some of Oshawott's most popular moves:

  • Tackle
  • Razor Shell
  • Aqua Jet
  • Water Pulse
  • Hydro Pump

Why should you care?
